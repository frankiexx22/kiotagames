Privacy Policy

Kiota Games

Introduction

Kiota Games is an independent game development studio based in Tanzania. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when you use our games, website, and related services.

Information We Collect

Most Kiota Games titles do not require players to create an account or directly provide personal information.

Depending on the game and services being used, certain technical information may be collected automatically by Google Play Services, Unity Services, Google AdMob, Unity LevelPlay (ironSource), and your device operating system.

This information may include device identifiers, advertising identifiers, operating system version, IP address, crash reports, gameplay statistics, and other diagnostic information used to improve our games and services.

Analytics and Crash Reports

Some Kiota Games titles use analytics and crash-reporting services to help improve game quality, stability, and performance.

Anonymous technical information that may be collected includes:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Crash reports
  • Performance statistics
  • Gameplay information
  • Diagnostic data

This information is used solely to identify issues, improve gameplay, and enhance the user experience.

Advertising

Some Kiota Games titles display advertisements provided by third-party advertising services, including Google AdMob and Unity LevelPlay (ironSource).

These advertising services may automatically collect certain information including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Approximate location
  • App interactions
  • Advertising performance and engagement data

This information may be used to deliver advertisements, measure advertising effectiveness, prevent fraudulent activity, and improve advertising services.

Third-Party Services

Our games may integrate third-party services to support game functionality, analytics, advertising, crash reporting, and application distribution.

These services may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Unity Gaming Services
  • Unity LevelPlay (ironSource)

These services operate independently and may process information according to their own privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

Kiota Games does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If we become aware that personal information has been collected without appropriate consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information.

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information collected through our games and services. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet can guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page together with the revised effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
