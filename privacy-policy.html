Privacy Policy

Kiota Games

Introduction

Kiota Games is an independent game development studio based in Tanzania. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when you use our games and services.

Information We Collect

Most Kiota Games titles do not require users to create accounts or provide personal information. We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information from players.

Some technical information may be collected automatically by platforms such as Google Play, Unity Services, or device operating systems to improve game performance and stability.

Analytics and Crash Reports

Our games may use analytics or crash-reporting services provided by third parties such as Unity or Google. These services may collect anonymous technical information including:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Game performance statistics
  • Crash reports
  • General gameplay information

This information helps us improve game quality and user experience.

Advertising

Currently, Kiota Games does not display third-party advertisements in its games.

In the future, we may introduce advertising services such as Google AdMob or similar advertising platforms. If advertising is added, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain how those services may collect and process information.

Children's Privacy

Kiota Games does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that personal information has been provided by a child without appropriate consent, we will take reasonable steps to remove such information.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information and maintain the security of our games and services. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any future updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
