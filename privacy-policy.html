Privacy Policy

Kiota Games

Introduction

Kiota Games is an independent game development studio based in Tanzania. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our games, website, and related services.

Information We Collect

Most Kiota Games titles do not require users to create accounts or directly provide personal information.

Depending on the game and services being used, certain technical information may be collected automatically by Google Play Services, Unity Services, Google AdMob, Unity LevelPlay (ironSource), and your device operating system.

This information may include device identifiers, operating system version, crash reports, gameplay statistics, advertising identifiers, IP address, and other diagnostic information used to improve our games and services.

Analytics and Crash Reports

Some Kiota Games titles use analytics and crash-reporting services provided by third parties such as Unity Technologies and Google.

These services may automatically collect anonymous technical information including:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Crash reports
  • Performance statistics
  • Gameplay information
  • Diagnostic data

This information helps us identify bugs, improve stability, and provide a better gaming experience.

Advertising

Some Kiota Games titles display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners including Google AdMob and Unity LevelPlay (ironSource).

These advertising providers may automatically collect certain information including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Approximate location
  • App interactions
  • Ad performance and engagement data

This information may be used to deliver relevant advertisements, measure advertising effectiveness, prevent fraud, and improve advertising services.

For more information, please review the privacy policies of our advertising partners:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Unity LevelPlay (ironSource) Privacy Policy

Third-Party Services

Our games may integrate third-party services that assist with game functionality, analytics, advertising, crash reporting, and distribution.

These services may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Unity Gaming Services
  • Unity LevelPlay (ironSource)

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Kiota Games does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If we become aware that such information has been collected without appropriate consent, we will take reasonable steps to remove it.

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect information collected through our games. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any future changes will be published on this page together with the updated effective date.
