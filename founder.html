Founder’s Vision

I built Kiota Games from the belief that where you begin should never determine how far you can go.

I started alone, with limited resources, learning, building, failing, rebuilding, and eventually publishing games from Tanzania for players around the world. But my vision has never been to remain a solo developer.

It is to build a studio where programmers and artists have the freedom to think, create, and turn their ideas into games that feel alive. A place where imagination is taken seriously, where people grow by building, and where good ideas are given the chance to become real.
