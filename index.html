About Kiota Games

Kiota Games is an independent game development studio based in Tanzania. We create mobile games across multiple genres including action, strategy, racing, puzzle, card and multiplayer experiences.


Our mission is to build fun, engaging games for players around the world while showcasing African creativity and innovation through game development.
